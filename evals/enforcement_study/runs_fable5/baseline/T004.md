Regulatory status of AI use in NIH grant applications:

According to NIH Guide Notice NOT-OD-25-132 (as paraphrased in the materials), NIH may reject applications that it determines were substantially developed by AI tools. This establishes that heavy reliance on AI to generate an application carries a risk of rejection, though the excerpt does not detail how NIH makes that determination.

Application cap per PI:

The cap is 6 applications per PI per calendar year, covering new, renewal, resubmission, and revision applications, effective September 25, 2025.
