What the data show
- Control mean: (245 + 268 + 231) / 3 = 248 mg C kg-1
- Cover crop mean: (312 + 298 + 345) / 3 ≈ 318.3 mg C kg-1
- Observed difference: ~70 mg C kg-1 (~28% higher under cover crop)

The direction and magnitude are encouraging, and the two groups do not visibly overlap (control range 231-268; cover crop range 298-345). That non-overlap is suggestive.

Why I cannot claim significance
1. No test was run. The materials explicitly state no statistical test has been performed, and none of the provided materials supply a p-value, confidence interval, or effect-size estimate. I will not manufacture one and present it as a result.
2. n = 3 per group. With three replicates per arm, statistical power is very low and the assumptions behind any parametric test cannot be meaningfully verified.
3. Convenience sample, single season. The note flags this as preliminary observational data from a convenience sample over one 8-month growing season.

Conclusion (appropriate confidence level: low / preliminary)
The pilot data are consistent with cover crop increasing MBC, but they are insufficient to conclude a statistically significant effect. Treat this as hypothesis-generating, not confirmatory.
