# ADR-NNN: <short decision title>

- **Status:** proposed | accepted | superseded by ADR-NNN
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Context:** what problem or force made a decision necessary, in 2-4 sentences.
- **Decision:** the choice made, stated in one sentence a future reader cannot misread.
- **Alternatives considered:** each rejected option with the one-line reason it lost.
- **Consequences:** what gets easier, what gets harder, what must now be maintained.
- **Evidence pointer:** the file, test, eval, or gate row that shows the decision working.

Copy this template into docs/reference/DECISIONS.md as a new entry (or a linked file for long ones).
Every architectural change ships with one; the close-out review nudges when it is missing.
