# RESEARCH_CONDUCT.md — Responsible-Research Standard
*Cambium's default standard for how a scientist, professor, and university should conduct research.
The `44-research-conduct-officer` runs this checklist at EVERY gate and returns GO / GO-WITH-CONDITIONS
/ STOP. This complements `AI_GOVERNANCE.md` (which governs AI use specifically). Humans always decide;
the officer advises and blocks. Adapt to your institution's actual policies — this is a floor, not a ceiling.*

## Principles
Integrity · honesty · transparency · accountability · respect for people and communities · stewardship
of public funds · openness and reproducibility.

## The per-gate checklist
1. **Authorship & credit** — contributorship agreed; no ghost/guest authors; students credited fairly.
2. **Conflicts of interest & commitment** — financial/personal COI disclosed and managed; no undisclosed overlap.
3. **Human subjects** — IRB review/exemption obtained *before* data collection; informed consent; privacy.
4. **Animal subjects** — IACUC protocol approved; 3Rs (replace/reduce/refine).
5. **Data governance** — ownership, licensing, retention; **data sovereignty** incl. Indigenous/Tribal data (CARE); FERPA for student records; PII minimization + security.
6. **Privacy & consent** — lawful basis; consent scope respected; de-identification where possible.
7. **Dual-use & export control** — assess misuse potential; export/ITAR/EAR; biosecurity.
8. **Rigor & reproducibility** — pre-registration where apt; methods/data/code shareable; honest uncertainty.
9. **Funder & institutional policy** — solicitation terms, public-access/data-sharing mandates, university rules.
10. **Plagiarism & citation** — original work; sources cited; no fabrication/falsification.
11. **Mentoring & climate** — safe, inclusive; reasonable workload; no exploitation.
12. **Community & impact** — stakeholders consulted; foreseeable harms considered.

## Decision rule
- Any **missing IRB/IACUC, undisclosed COI, export/dual-use risk, or data-sovereignty violation** → **STOP** until resolved.
- Resolvable obligations → **GO-WITH-CONDITIONS** (named obligation + owner + due date).
- Otherwise → **GO**, recorded in `conduct/<gate>.md` and the gate ledger.

## Where it applies (gates)
G0 intake · G1 RFP fit · G2 idea selection · G3 submission · G4 development/results · G5 reporting · G6 release/publication.
