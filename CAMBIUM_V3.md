# Cambium v3 — the full research lifecycle (AI-heavy, human-governed)
*v3 extends Cambium from "win a proposal and verify results" to the **entire program lifecycle** — from
watching funding calls, through team-built submissions, to post-award execution, reporting, and
feedback — with **responsible-research governance at every gate**. Built by running Cambium's own method
on Cambium itself.*

## New agents (39 → 45)
| Agent | Council | Tier | Adds |
|---|---|---|---|
| `39-rfp-radar` | Pre-Award | sonnet | ingest a funding call + **score fit vs your USER_PROFILE** → targetable areas |
| `40-convener` | Partnerships | sonnet | organize Co-PIs into a **shared pre-award workspace**; section owners + register |
| `41-budget-officer` | Pre-Award | sonnet | line-item **budget + justification** to the solicitation's cost rules |
| `42-grants-compliance` | Pre-Award | sonnet | biosketch, DMP, current&pending, facilities, **RFP-specific forms** |
| `43-feedback-router` | Support | sonnet | split human feedback into items, **route each to the right fixer**, track to close |
| `44-research-conduct-officer` | Governance | opus | **responsible-research checklist at every gate** (IRB/IACUC, COI, FERPA, data sovereignty, dual-use…) |

## New documents & templates
- `USER_PROFILE.md` (template) — so Cambium knows *who you are* before it brainstorms.
- `IDEA_INBOX.md` (template) — a space for your own / external-AI ideas to enter the funnel.
- `COLLAB_WORKSPACE.md` (template) — the shared room where a team co-writes a proposal.
- `POST_AWARD_PLAN.md` (template) — assignments, groups, **scoped agent access**, and the alert map.
- `RESEARCH_CONDUCT.md` — the default responsible-research standard + per-gate decision rule.
- `LIFECYCLE_V3.md` — the end-to-end map (stages, agents, gates, triggers).

## New gate
**G0 — Know the PI:** Cambium won't brainstorm until a `USER_PROFILE` exists, so ideas are grounded in
your real expertise and the RFP fit is real, not generic.

## Governance everywhere
Three independent checks run at each gate: **research conduct** (this is new), **evidence integrity**
(claim tiers), and **AI use** (`AI_GOVERNANCE.md`). An open STOP halts the phase.

## Roster balance
45 agents across 10 functions; the additions are one governance officer plus five coordinators/doers
that cover the parts of a real program Cambium previously left to the human alone.
