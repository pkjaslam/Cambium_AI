## Background
Campus leadership wants to right-size the bike-share fleet, reduce rebalancing costs, and improve
rider satisfaction. Current allocation relies on manual counts and intuition. A data-driven demand
forecaster could enable proactive redistribution.

## Priorities
1. Accurate short-horizon demand forecasts (1–24 hours) at station level.
2. Interpretable outputs usable by non-technical operations staff.
3. Open code and documented methodology.
4. Respect for rider privacy (no individual tracking).
