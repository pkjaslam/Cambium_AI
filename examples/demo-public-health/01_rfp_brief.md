# 01 RFP Brief — Community Wastewater Surveillance for Infectious Disease Early Warning (FICTIONAL DEMO)
*Agent: RFP-Analyst | Date: demo | Input: RFP.md*

## Sponsor & ceiling
Fictional State Public Health Research Fund (SPHRF) · $210 K / 30 months ·
evaluation: significance/equity 30 % / lab rigour 25 % / community engagement 25 % / feasibility 20 %.

## Must-haves (disqualifying if missed)
- Site selection explicitly centred on underserved / equity-priority communities.
- Validated laboratory method for low-flow / decentralised wastewater systems.
- Community-facing reporting mechanism designed without assuming clinical infrastructure.
- Evaluation framework covering epidemiological sensitivity AND community trust.
- Letter(s) of support from at least one community-based organisation (CBO) partner — required.

## Fit assessment
| Criterion | Our capacity | Gap |
|---|---|---|
| Public-health significance & equity | Strong — Environmental Health group has rural-health equity track record | Need to identify specific partner communities |
| Lab method (WBE, low-flow) | Medium — standard WBE experience; low-flow adaptations require protocol development | 3–4 months method-development phase needed |
| Community engagement | Weak without a CBO partner | **Action required: identify and approach CBO partner before LOI** |
| Feasibility / team | Strong — Env. Health PI + Epidemiology Co-PI available | Budget may be tight for 4-site pilot; check scope |

**Overall fit: 7 / 10 (rises to 9/10 if CBO partner secured).**
